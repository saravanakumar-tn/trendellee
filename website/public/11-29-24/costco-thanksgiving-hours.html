Costco Thanksgiving Hours: Is Costco Open on Thanksgiving?

Nov 29, 2024 2 minutes read

Thanksgiving is a time for family, friends, and... maybe a little shopping? If you're wondering about Costco's Thanksgiving hours, you're not alone! Many people are curious about store hours on this major holiday.

Unfortunately for those hoping for a post-turkey Costco run, Costco is typically closed on Thanksgiving Day. They prioritize giving their employees time with their families.

So, no, Costco is usually NOT open on Thanksgiving. This has been consistent for several years, so don't expect a last-minute change.

What about Walmart?

Many people also search for Walmart's Thanksgiving hours. Walmart's hours vary by location, so it's always best to check your local store's hours online or by calling them directly. While some Walmarts may have limited hours on Thanksgiving, many are open.

Planning Ahead

If you have a Costco shopping list that can't wait, make sure to get your errands done before Thanksgiving Day! The best way to confirm is to check the Costco store locator on their website and input your specific location. For Walmart, do the same. They usually have online store locators as well.

Remember, holiday hours can change, so always double-check with the store directly before heading out.

Happy Thanksgiving!
