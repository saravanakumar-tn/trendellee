Staying Safe During a Blowing Snow Advisory

  • Check the weather forecast regularly: Stay informed about the latest updates and warnings.
  • Limit your travel: Only go out if absolutely necessary.
  • Drive slowly and cautiously: Increase your following distance significantly.
  • Be aware of wind chill: Dress warmly in layers.
  • Keep an emergency kit in your vehicle: This should include blankets, food, water, and a flashlight.
