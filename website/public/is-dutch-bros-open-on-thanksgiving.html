Is Dutch Bros Open on Thanksgiving? Your Holiday Coffee Conundrum Solved!

Nov 28, 2024 1 minute read

Thanksgiving's around the corner, and for many, that means family, food, and... coffee! But what about your Dutch Bros fix? Will those delicious Rebel energy drinks be flowing on Turkey Day? Let's dive in and find out.

Unfortunately, the answer isn't a simple yes or no. Dutch Bros locations are independently owned and operated. This means their Thanksgiving hours vary widely from store to store. Some locations might be open with adjusted hours, while others might be completely closed for the holiday to allow their employees a well-deserved break.

How to Find Your Local Dutch Bros Thanksgiving Hours:

Your best bet is to check the Dutch Bros app or their website. Most locations will post their special holiday hours in advance. You can also try searching online for "[Your City/Town] Dutch Bros Thanksgiving Hours" or giving your local store a quick call.

Don't rely on past years' hours, as things can change! Make sure you check directly with your specific location for the most accurate information before heading out for your caffeine fix.
