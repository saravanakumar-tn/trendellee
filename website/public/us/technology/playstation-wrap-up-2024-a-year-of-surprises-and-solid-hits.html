PlayStation Wrap-Up 2024: A Year of Surprises and Solid Hits

Dec 11, 2024 5 minutes read

Wow, what a year for PlayStation! 2024 absolutely delivered, throwing us some curveballs and satisfying long-time fans with some highly anticipated titles. Let's dive into the highlights, shall we?

The Blockbusters: No doubt, Marvel's Spider-Man 3 was a massive hit, selling millions of copies and setting new records. The game delivered on its promise, offering stunning visuals and an epic story. It wasn't all superheroes though. Horizon Forbidden West: Burning Shores also delivered another stunning adventure, expanding the lore and world in beautiful ways.

The Unexpected Gems: Sometimes, the smaller games steal the show. Inscryption completely blew us away. Its unique gameplay and charming art style quickly became a fan favorite, proving that indie development is alive and kicking. We also saw a resurgence of the narrative-driven adventure game with the release of Hollow Knight, showcasing that there's still room for innovation in familiar genres.

The PlayStation Plus Advantage: PlayStation Plus continued to offer a solid lineup of free games throughout the year, providing great value for subscribers. Some months were definitely better than others, but overall, it was a win for PS Plus members who got to explore a diverse range of titles.

Looking Ahead to 2025: Rumors are swirling about a new PlayStation console, potentially a mid-cycle refresh, or maybe even something even more exciting. While nothing's confirmed, the speculation alone keeps the excitement going! We also anticipate continued strong support for the PS5 with more big-budget titles and exciting indie releases. It's going to be another busy year!

Overall, 2024 was a strong year for PlayStation. It demonstrated the platform's ability to deliver AAA experiences while still nurturing a vibrant indie scene. Here's to hoping 2025 is just as exciting!
